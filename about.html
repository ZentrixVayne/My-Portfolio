Who Am I?

I'm Arshman Anil, a passionate web and mobile app designer and developer from Karachi, Pakistan. Currently studying in Matric (Class 10) at SMS Aga Khan School, I've discovered my love for creating digital experiences that combine beautiful design with functional code.

My journey in web development started at a young age when I became curious about how websites work. What began as a hobby quickly turned into a passion as I delved deeper into HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Today, I'm proficient in modern frameworks like React and have a keen eye for UI/UX design.

I believe in the power of clean code and intuitive design. Every project I work on is an opportunity to learn something new and push my creative boundaries. When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new design trends, contributing to open-source projects, or working on my own creative experiments.
